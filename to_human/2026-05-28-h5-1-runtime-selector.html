Calibration Signal

The best variant differs across contexts: B512_W4 for compute/large, B8192_W8 for compute/small and memory/large, B4096_W4 for idle/large, B2048_W8 for idle/small, and B1024_W4 for memory/small. Worst-to-best spread ranges from 11.8% to 74.4%.

Profiler Evidence

The Nsight Systems smoke profile generated nsys_h5_1_tooling_smoke.nsys-rep and .sqlite. CUDA API time was dominated by cudaLaunchKernel and cuLibraryLoadData, while the fused GPU kernel work was only hundreds of microseconds in total. That supports the next step: quantify launch gaps, cache-hit overhead, and submission jitter directly.
