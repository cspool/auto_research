Conclusion

H5.4 supports the overhead story: warmed execution removes Triton cold-start noise, but a Python/PyTorch stream queue still spends enough host-side work on events, waits, and launches that sub-2% selector gains are fragile. The next useful step is graph replay or persistent/batched dispatch, then task migration on a cheaper substrate.
